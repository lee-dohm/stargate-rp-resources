# Terminology

## Character sheet abbreviations

* **AK:** Area Knowledge
* **HKA:** Killing Attack - Hand-to-Hand
* **KS:** Knowledge Skill
* **PS:** Professional Skill
* **RKA:** Killing Attack - Ranged
* **SS:** Science Skill
